Education System

School’s Motto

Strive hard in Allah’s cause as you ought to strive.


Our Vision

To facilitate holistic education through a conducive and competitive learner friendly environment with resourceful faculties and advanced infrastructural facilities.


Our Mission

Empower the students with quality education, instill high standard of intellectual capacities and Islamic values.


Our Philosophy of Education

  • To provide functional literacy for all school aged children at Maroshi.
  • To instill the Islamic and social values required to become productive and contributing citizens of the island and the nation.
  • To contribute to sustain Sh. Maroshi as a drug and violence free island giving proper education and awareness to the younger generation.
  • To guide the students to develop high self confidence and self esteem.
  • To facilitate the students to be responsible learns.
  • To set the students on the path of life-long learning.
