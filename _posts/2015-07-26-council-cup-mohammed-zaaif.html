Council Cup 2015

written by Mohamed Zaaif, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

At first school played against fenaka and school won. The score was school 5 goals and fenaka 1. To scool next match was to against council. In this match also school won the match and at last in the final also school won the match.
