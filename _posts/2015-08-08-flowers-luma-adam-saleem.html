Flowers are Like Us

written by Luma Adam Saleem, Grade 8, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

Flower is a thing which people mostly like. Most of the people like the color of the flower, nd most of the people like the smell. In every occasion or in every festival or in parties or every wedding everyone is using flower. mostly in india people use flowers.

Flower is simlilar to human being. Every flower is small and the petals are not opened when they are produced. When we human being also are born we are not able to walk at first. We grow by day by year a little like this the flower is also coming as alittle flower nd it is growing by day by week a little. Each flower has it on color like that we humanbeing also have our own beauty.

Flower also die as well as we human being also die. We also dn't know when we will die as well as flower also dn't know when they will die. flower has a short life time and as well as us humanbeing also have a short life time and some of the human being might have long life time.
