First Day of School

written by Jumaana, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

At 2 August 2015 we came school after a big hoilday. Because in Maldives there was dengue fever. We get more then 30 days for holiday. After 6 days teacher gave hoilday assignments. Everyday we are doing the assignments. In that hoilday some of family went to picnic and some another island. After so many day we came school and its so much fun. We met our friends after so many days so we share what we do in the hoilday and what funny things happened. We were very excited to meet our friends. When we came to school we saw three new faces. After 2 days we saw one more new face.

Jumaana, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school
