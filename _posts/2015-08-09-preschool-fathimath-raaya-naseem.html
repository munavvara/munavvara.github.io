Our Preschool

written by Fathimath Raaya Naseem, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

In preschool there are three classes. They are LKG (Lower Kindergrten), UKG (Upper Kindergarten) and Nursery. In Nursery there are 6 students, in LKG there are 7 sudents and in UKG there are 6 students. That is total 19 students. There is only one teacher to teach all these three classes. UKG students will come to the schoool at 6:50 and they leave the school at 9:25. LKG students come to the school at 10:23 and they leave the school at 12:30 and nursery students come to the school at 12:40 and their classes end at 1:45. Their class is decorated in a very child friendly way so that they can enjoy their studies. Along with a class room there is a play ground within the campus. The students in thepreschool are very cute and friendly. Sometimes volunteers come to teach the preschool students.

Fathimath Raaya Naseem, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school
