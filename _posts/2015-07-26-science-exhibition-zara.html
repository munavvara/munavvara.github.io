Science Exhibition 2014

written by Zara, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

There was Science exhibition in our School in 2014. Science exhibition was held in September. This was held in after so many days of hard working. In this exhibition there was many activities, such as explaining the importance of our body. The all islanders present here. The in charge of this exhibition is Ahmed Sir and Jisha miss. To held this exhibition many Islanders, Teachers and School students helped a lot. Some students took Shells from the sea for this exhibition. We made Short eats to sell in the exhibition and we brought different types of trees to sell. There were different areas for different activities.

First of all, the first area there was Black Corals, Mushroom Corals etc. Black Corals are are a group of deep water, tree-like corals related to sea anemones. They are also found in rare dark shallow water areas such as Newzealans's Milford Sound where they can be viewed from an underwater observatory or Via SCUBA driving. In the exhibition we explained the functions of different parts of our body. We explained the functions of Kidney to the students. We also explain the function of Enzymes. We make large structures of Kidneys, digestive system enzymes and many more by the use of different materials for example wood and clay. We get the help from senior students, parents and teachers.

Zara, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school
