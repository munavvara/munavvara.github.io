Childrens Day 2015

written by Shihaadh Nasyr, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school

Children's Day is celebrated in Maldives every year on May 10. This holiday is awaited by children as well as by their parents and teachers. Celebration of Children's Day in Maldives is one of the ways to make adults aware about their responsibility towards children and their happy early years. The festive events scatter across the country, making every school and kindergarten participate. Every school organizes different activities for children. The classes are decorated to create a party atmosphere. Teachers make up special activities for children, for instance, class party, games, competitions.

We were invited to the assembly. We finished our assembly by the Children's day cake cutting. And at the evening we had a special childrens evening at the school .Many stalls were presented from different institutions of the island. Many presents were gifted to the childrens. Chidrens was playing the games in the evening. About 8 stalls were preseted at the evenings.

Not only this year, every year our school organises many activities to celebrate the Childrens day. School teachers, senior students,parents and the members of the institutions works very hard in the preparations of the children's day activities and various performances by school children dressed in colorful costumes.

Shihaadh Nasyr, Grade 9, Al Madhrasathul Munavvara school
