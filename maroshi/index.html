The Island of Maroshi

Name: Maroshi Island
Atoll: Shaviyani Atoll, also called as (Miladhummadulhu)
Island Code: C-10
Location Of Island: (Distance from Male): 230 km (88 miles)
Population: over 800 people
About the People: People of Maroshi are very kind and very welcoming.
How to Get to the Island: There is scheduled speed boat ferries operated to Raa. Alifushi and from Alifushi a separate transfer is taken to Maroshi. The whole journey could take about 5 hours
Alternative Transport Option: By seaplane to Vice Roy.
Nearby Resort: Vice Roy Hotels, Resorts Residences Maldives
Remarks: The atoll capital of Shaviyani atoll is Funadhoo. Though Maroshi population is over 800 people many people are working in Male' the capital and in resorts near and far from the island and because of this we find few people living on the island but on holiday period you may find many people on the island as they all come for holidays.
