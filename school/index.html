Al Madharusathul Munavvara

Al Madhurasatul Munavvara is situated in Maroshi island of Shaviyani Atoll, Republic of Maldives. It is located in one of the most beautiful islands of Maldives nestled in a lush green area with marvellous, clean sandy beach surronded by the Indian Ocean.

The school was founded as a private primary school in 1978 by Mr. Hussain Abdulla and in 1996 it turned into a secondary school. The students from nearby islands also joined the school because of its reputation. The school was handed over to the Ministry of Education in 2007.

Modern and up to date facilities are provided to complement the quality of education at Al Madhurasathul Munavvara.

The school has a beautiful sprawling campus that houses the Nursery, the Primary, the middle and the Secondary Schools. The campus is spread over the total land area of approximately 2 acres. It shares the community playground organising various games and sports. The school also consists of a large multi-purpose auditorium.

The class rooms are precious, well ventilated with plenty of space for the students and the teachers to move around and interact. Each classroom is well furnished with television and white board where teachers deliver lessons using latest educational technologies such as power point presentations, internet references,etc.

The school is well known for its highly qualified and well experienced staff who constantly strive for excellence and for the development of all round personality of each student.

The school is a source of pride not only for the island or atoll, but for the whole country also, for maintaining an international standard in all aspects, for example, A SMART classroom to chase the technology, a broad-band connectivity to be a part of the global village.

We promise to be unique!
