Munavvara

The school in Maroshi named Al Madhrasathul Munavvara was built by a wealthy businessman from Maroshi Hussain Abdulla. Before year 2007, the school was a private school and after the school was handed to the local government, so now it is run by the government. The school includes one preschool, a first to sixth grade Primary school and seventh to tenth secondary school which students could complete up to GCE O' level.
